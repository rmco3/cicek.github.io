New Version: v2.0.5 BETA!

26.04.2025

CicekPlayer v2.0.5 is here! This is a BETA release that introduces a multitude of exciting new features, improvements, and fixes. Enhancements include improved search functionality for music and playlists, integration of new search engines, tagging capabilities, advanced settings, a sleep timer, music conversion tools, mini mode, image customization, smart shuffle, automatic playlist creation, music rating and customization options, an ambient mode, auto-pause/resume with other media, and a revamped music info window. The underlying libraries have been updated, and there are major UI changes, performance improvements, and bug fixes.
