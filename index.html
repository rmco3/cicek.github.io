New Version: v2.0.5!

CicekPlayer v2.0.5 is here! This significant update brings a host of new features and improvements. Organize your music better with new tagging capabilities and multiple music search modes. Dive into advanced settings, set a sleep timer, and convert your music with the new CicekPlayer Converter.
