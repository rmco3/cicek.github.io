New Version: v2.0.7 - Feature-Packed Update!

July 26, 2025

We are thrilled to announce the release of CicekPlayer v2.0.7, packed with exciting new features and significant improvements! Enjoy an even more refined and faster search experience. Discover our new automatic music deletion suggestions (an optional feature) to keep your library tidy. Music playback now intelligently auto-pauses/resumes when you press Win+L or your PC goes to sleep (also optional). For those who love consistency, your last search will now automatically load when the application opens – perfect for resuming your "cute cat searchs"! We've also added convenient settings export/import functionality and a brand new Music Statistics window, where you can view your most listened music, albums, and artists, and even download your data. As always, this update includes various bug fixes, library updates, and general performance enhancements for an even smoother experience.
